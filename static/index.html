Every portfolio tracker I tried either lied to me about returns (averaging post-deposit), ignored my French tax envelopes, or made me eyeball the chart vs the S&P. So I built one that doesn't.

  • PEA / CTO / AV / PER side-by-side tax sim in one click.

  • Monthly review that reads your portfolio like a senior advisor.
