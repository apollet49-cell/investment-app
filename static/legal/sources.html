Fine print · 02

Where the numbers come from.

Every figure in InvestApp can be traced to a public source. No proprietary feeds, no black-box "alternative data". If something looks wrong, you can audit it.

Market data

Yahoo Finance Live, primary
Stock, ETF and crypto prices, historical OHLC, dividend history, company calendars. Accessed via the open yfinance Python client.
finance.yahoo.com
Alpha Vantage Fallback
Used when a Yahoo Finance ticker fails or rate-limits. Free tier, 5 requests per minute.
alphavantage.co
European Central Bank Forex
Reference exchange rates for EUR pairs. Daily fixings, published 16:00 CET.
ecb.europa.eu/stats/eurofxref
Open Exchange Rates Forex backup
USD-based forex when ECB doesn't cover the pair. Free tier.
openexchangerates.org

French real estate

DVF — Demandes de Valeurs Foncières Live, primary
Open dataset of every real-estate transaction registered in France since 2014. Source of the median €/m² used for valuation. Accessed via the api.cquest.org/dvf public endpoint with a ±25 % surface filter and a 90 % outlier trim.
data.gouv.fr/dvf
INSEE — Institut national de la statistique Reference
French statistics institute. Used as the reference for inflation deflators and population-level benchmarks in the FIRE projection.
insee.fr

Macro & benchmark

FRED — Federal Reserve Economic Data Reference
Risk-free rate (3-month T-bill) used in the Sharpe ratio computation. Updated daily.
fred.stlouisfed.org
S&P 500 (^GSPC) Benchmark
Default benchmark for the "are you beating the market" comparison. Historical price series via Yahoo Finance, normalised to 100 at the first snapshot.
finance.yahoo.com/quote/^GSPC

Regulatory framework

AMF — Autorité des marchés financiers Context
French financial regulator. Reference for the tax envelope rules implemented in the simulator (PEA, CTO, AV, PER). Their public guides on the fiscalité de l'épargne are the authoritative source for the rates used.
amf-france.org

Optional AI layer

Anthropic Claude Optional
Used to draft the prose section of the Monthly Review when the user supplies their own API key. The deterministic, rule-based review works without it.
anthropic.com
